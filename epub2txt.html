EPUB to TXT

Extract plain text from an EPUB file in reading order.

About & limitations

What this does: Reads the EPUB's container.xml to find the OPF package, walks the spine in reading order, and extracts visible text from each XHTML chapter. <script> and <style> content is dropped; block elements become paragraph breaks; chapters are separated by a blank line.

What it cannot do: DRM-protected EPUBs (Adobe ADEPT, Apple FairPlay, Kindle KFX) cannot be read — they are encrypted and this tool does not break DRM. Images, tables, footnotes, and ruby annotations are stripped down to their text content.

Privacy: Everything runs in your browser. Your file never leaves your device.

All data stays on your device.
